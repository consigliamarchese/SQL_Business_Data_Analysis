## 1. Product & Employee Insights
File: `01_SQL_Product_Employee_Insights.sql`

Queries include:

## 2. Sales & Revenue Analysis
File: `02_SQL_Sales_Revenue_Profit_Analysis.sql`

Queries include:

- Verifying primary keys
- Counting daily transactions
- Total revenue, quantity, average unit price per product
- Revenue and quantity per product category
- Revenue per city exceeding 60K
